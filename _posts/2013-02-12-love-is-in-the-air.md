Peter kept describing it as “you know…it’s a city that’s fun.”&#160; And fun it was, or at least the kids had fun.&#160; I would have had more fun with a little more caffeine maybe.&#160; Starbucks should definitely be handed out to every parent as they walk in the door at those places.&#160;
