<p>And in keeping with the ridiculously late blog posts I guess it’s finally time to post about Gabby’s Kindergarten Celebration.&#160; After all, next week she’ll be a 1st grader.&#160; I can’t believe it!&#160; So not ready for that.&#160; And really not ready for Summer to be over but Gabby is excited to start her new year.&#160; </p>  <p>Anyway…below are pictures from the Celebration night.&#160; The kids dressed up and got to perform on stage at the Arend Arts Center at the Bentonville High School.&#160; I brought our video camera but forgot to erase all of the previous recordings so we ran out of room and didn’t get a video of any of the songs.&#160; But we did get a picture of Gabby getting her Citizenship Award!&#160; We were so proud of her!!&#160; 2 students from each class were selected for the award and our sweet Gabby girl was one of the 2 from her class.&#160; The award goes to someone who has been a good example for the rest of the class with behavior and attitude.&#160;
